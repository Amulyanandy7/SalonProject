Haircut Details

Buzz Cut A low-maintenance cut that requires minimal styling. A good option for active boys or those who prefer a no-fuss style.

Crew Cut A classic cut that's short on the sides and back, with a slightly longer top. A low-maintenance style that looks clean and put-together.

Undercut A masculine cut that contrasts the top of the hair with the sides and back. Can be styled in multiple ways and has a youthful appearance.

Faux Hawk A trendy cut that mimics the mohawk but is less dramatic. Has shorter sides and a strip of longer hair from front to back.

Mullet A dynamic fusion of long boy haircuts and short boy haircuts. Often incorporates fade haircuts for boys.

Side Part A traditional style that involves cutting the sides and top of the head. A style that has stood the test of time.

Shaving Details

Wet Shaving A traditional method of shaving that uses a safety razor or straight razor. Involves lathering the skin with soap or shaving cream before shaving.

Dry Shaving Uses an electric razor, which has rotating blades that don't require shaving cream, soap, or water.

Safety Razor An old-fashioned razor that uses a protective guard to reduce the risk of cuts. A good option for people with sensitive skin.

Straight Razor A knife blade sharpened on a stone to create a sharp wedge. A traditional option that some prefer. Used by professional barbers to create natural texture at the ends of hair.

Electrolysis A hair removal method that should only be performed by a board-certified dermatologist or electrologist. Works best when hair follicles are in the growth phase.

Massage Details

Swedish Massage A relaxing massage that involves long strokes, kneading, and tapping to promote circulation and ease muscle tension.

Deep Tissue Massage A therapeutic massage that targets the deeper layers of muscle and connective tissue to help relieve pain and stiffness.

Sports Massage A specialized massage designed for athletes and individuals who engage in physical activity, helping to improve flexibility, reduce muscle soreness, and enhance performance.

Shiatsu Massage A Japanese massage technique that applies pressure to specific points on the body to balance the flow of energy and promote relaxation.

Aromatherapy Massage A massage that incorporates essential oils to promote relaxation, reduce stress, and improve mood.

Hot Stone Massage A massage that uses heated stones to relax muscles, improve circulation, and reduce tension.

Facial Details

Deep Cleansing Facial A customized facial that deeply cleanses the pores, reducing acne and impurities, and leaving the skin feeling refreshed and revitalized.

Anti-Aging Facial A facial designed to target fine lines, wrinkles, and age spots, using advanced skincare techniques and products to promote collagen production and skin elasticity.

Sensitive Skin Facial A gentle, soothing facial tailored for sensitive skin, using fragrance-free and hypoallergenic products to calm and comfort the skin.

Brightening Facial A facial that targets dull, uneven skin tone, using advanced brightening agents and exfoliating techniques to reveal a brighter, more radiant complexion.

Acne Facial A customized facial designed to target acne-prone skin, using advanced skincare techniques and products to reduce inflammation, prevent breakouts, and promote clear skin.

Facial Treatment Details

Microdermabrasion A non-invasive exfoliating treatment that removes dead skin cells, smoothens out fine lines and wrinkles, and improves skin texture.

Chemical Peel A treatment that uses a solution to remove the top layers of the skin, reducing the appearance of fine lines, wrinkles, and hyperpigmentation.

Facial Steaming A treatment that uses steam to open up the pores, loosen blackheads and whiteheads, and improve skin clarity.

Extraction Facial A treatment that involves manually removing blackheads, whiteheads, and other impurities from the skin, leaving it feeling clean and refreshed.

LED Light Therapy A non-invasive treatment that uses different wavelengths of light to target various skin concerns, such as acne, aging, and hyperpigmentation.

Microneedling A minimally invasive treatment that uses tiny needles to create micro-injuries in the skin, stimulating collagen production and improving skin texture.
